About

Senior mobile engineer with a fintech-first lens.

I architect and deliver high-performance native and cross-platform applications — with a track record across payments, lending, savings, and crypto.

For the last five years, I've focused on building secure, user-centric Android applications in Java and Kotlin, while leveraging Flutter and Kotlin Multiplatform for scalable hybrid development.

My career has been rooted in fintech — from EMV-compliant POS terminals at Itex, to a Flutter rebuild of the Bundle (Binance) crypto exchange, to architecting Alerzo Pay POS from scratch, and now driving the Savings squad at FairMoney.

I care deeply about clean architecture, app performance, mentoring teammates through thoughtful code review, and turning complex business requirements into intuitive mobile experiences.
